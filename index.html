Welcome to Karnal HighSchool

Welcome to Karnal Highschool, a cornerstone of learning and community in the Miraj block since 1964. With a proud legacy of providing quality, co-educational instruction in the Marathi medium, we are dedicated to nurturing the potential within every student from Grade 5 to 10. Our school provides a safe and supportive environment where students are encouraged to thrive. From our well-maintained classrooms and a library rich with over 2,400 books to a playground for physical development, we focus on the holistic growth of our students. We are committed to their well-being, ensuring a healthy learning atmosphere with amenities like clean drinking water and a nourishing mid-day meal prepared on-site. By integrating modern tools like computers into our teaching, we strive to build a strong academic foundation while fostering curiosity, character, and the skills needed to succeed in the future.
