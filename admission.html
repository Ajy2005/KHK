Admission Process

A Warm Welcome to Prospective Students

We appreciate your interest in Karnal HighSchool. Our school is dedicated to fostering a supportive and engaging learning community. The admission process is straightforward and designed to help us understand your child's needs. We look forward to welcoming new members to our school family.

Key Admission Dates

Event Date
Distribution of Admission Forms From March 1st, 2025
Last Date for Submission of Forms April 15th, 2025
Display of Selected Candidates List April 30th, 2025
Last Date for Fee Payment May 15th, 2025

How to Apply

  1. Collect the admission form from the school office between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM on working days.
  2. Fill in the application form with complete and accurate information.
  3. Attach all the required documents with the form.
  4. Submit the completed form at the school office before the due date.

Eligibility

Admissions are available for students from Grade 5 to Grade 10. The candidate must have successfully passed the final examinations of the preceding class from a recognized school.

Documents Required

  • Photocopy of the student's Birth Certificate.
  • Original School Leaving Certificate (from the previous school).
  • Photocopy of the previous year's academic report card.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs of the student.
  • Photocopy of the student's Aadhaar Card.
